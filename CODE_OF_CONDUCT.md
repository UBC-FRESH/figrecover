# Code Of Conduct

`figrecover` is a UBC-FRESH open-source research software project. Project
spaces should remain professional, respectful, and focused on useful technical
work.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful of collaborators, reviewers, users, and maintainers.
- Critique ideas, code, evidence, and assumptions rather than people.
- Keep discussions constructive and relevant to the project.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality around unpublished documents, private
  data, and generated recovery outputs.
- Disclose conflicts, uncertainty, and limitations when they affect technical
  decisions.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, or discriminatory conduct.
- Publishing private PDFs, recovered private tables, prompt logs, review
  bundles, or unpublished data without explicit approval.
- Misrepresenting approximate recovered values as authoritative source data.
- Repeatedly derailing review, issue, or pull-request discussions.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the project maintainers through the repository issue tracker
when appropriate, or through UBC-FRESH lab leadership channels when the concern
should not be public.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, restrict participation, or take
other proportionate action to keep project spaces professional and safe.
